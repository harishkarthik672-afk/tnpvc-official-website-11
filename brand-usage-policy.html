Brand Usage Policy

Guidelines for using TNPVC Council's brand assets and trademarks.

Using the TNPVC Logo

The TNPVC logo is a registered trademark. You may use our logo only after obtaining written permission from our management team.

  • Do not alter the logo's proportions or colors.
  • Ensure sufficient clear space around the logo.
  • Do not use the logo in a way that implies endorsement without approval.

Typography and Colors

Our brand uses specific typography (Plus Jakarta Sans) and colors (TNPVC Purple, Red, and Blue). Maintaining consistency helps strengthen our brand identity.

Authorized Partners

Verified TNPVC partners and members are granted limited rights to use the "Verified Partner" badge on their marketing materials, subject to our compliance standards.

Questions?

If you have any questions regarding the use of our brand assets, please reach out to us.
