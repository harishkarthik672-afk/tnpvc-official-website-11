Using the TNPVC Logo

The TNPVC Official Logo is the core of our visual identity. It represents the trust and standard Srinivasan has built across Tamil Nadu. Use is restricted to authorized projects and affiliated members only.

  • Maintain original proportions; do not stretch or distort.
  • Ensure clear exclusion zone around the logo.
  • Use only on approved backgrounds (Navy, White, or Red).
  • Do not create derivative logos or combined marks without council approval.

Typography & Colors

To maintain a premium feel, all TNPVC digital and print communications must use our designated typeface, Plus Jakarta Sans, and our primary palette: TNPVC Navy (#1b0f3a) and TNPVC Red (#e3001b).

Verified Partner Badges

Verified service providers are granted a limited license to display the "TNPVC Verified Contractor" badge on their personal social media and storefronts. This badge is a mark of compliance and cannot be leased or transferred.

Brand Inquiries

If you have questions about brand implementation or wish to request vector assets for a verified project, please reach out to our communications hub.
