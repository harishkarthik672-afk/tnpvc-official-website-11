Who We Are

TNPVC is a Tamil Nadu based uPVC and PVC association that connects homeowners, builders, contractors, fabricators, and suppliers under one trusted network.

Why We Started

We started TNPVC to bring quality standards, verified teams, and transparent execution to uPVC/PVC projects, so customers get reliable results without confusion.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to deliver durable uPVC/PVC solutions through skilled execution and honest service. Our vision is to build Tamil Nadu's most dependable network for modern door, window, and interior panel works.
