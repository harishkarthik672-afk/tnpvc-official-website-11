Data Protection

All data submitted through TNPVC Council platforms is protected by high-standard encryption. We utilize HTTPS with TLS 1.3 for all web connections to ensure your project details and contact info are secured in transit across Tamil Nadu's digital landscape.

Secure Infrastructure

Our server environment is maintained with constant security patching and vulnerability assessments. We use firewalls and intrusion monitoring to protect the identities of our verified contractors and material suppliers.

HTTPS / TLS 1.3 AES-256 Storage Real-time Monitoring

Access Control

Access to sensitive data is strictly limited to authorized council personnel and verified project partners only. We implement a "zero-trust" approach to ensure no unauthorized entity can access worker profiles or site images.

Reporting Issues

If you encounter any security anomaly on our platform, please report it immediately to our technical compliance team. We address all security inquiries with high priority to maintain the trust of our state-wide community.
