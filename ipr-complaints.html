What is IPR?

Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) encompass trademarks, logos, brand names, visual assets, and exclusive technical documentation created by the TNPVC Council. These rights are protected under Indian intellectual property laws to ensure the uniqueness of our state-wide service portal.

Protected Assets

Unauthorized use of the following council assets is strictly prohibited:

  • TNPVC Council name, logo, and brand identity
  • Website architecture, source code, and curated text
  • Proprietary project verification processes
  • Database of verified contractors and workers
  • Exclusive industry reports and newsletters

Filing a Complaint

If you believe your intellectual property has been infringed upon within the TNPVC ecosystem, or if you suspect unauthorized use of TNPVC assets, please submit a formal notice. Our legal compliance team reviews all IPR inquiries to maintain platform integrity.
