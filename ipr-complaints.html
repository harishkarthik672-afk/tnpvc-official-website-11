IPR Complaints

Intellectual Property Rights protection policies for TNPVC Council.

What is IPR?

Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) cover trademarks, logos, brand names, images, content, and designs created by TNPVC Council. Unauthorized use of our intellectual property is not permitted.

Protected Content

  • TNPVC Council name, logo, and visual identity
  • Website content, text, and images
  • Marketing materials and promotional content
  • Project documentation and reports
  • Trade secrets and business processes

Filing a Complaint

If you believe your intellectual property has been infringed upon by TNPVC or any party affiliated with TNPVC, you may submit a formal complaint through our Contact page.

Please include: a description of the IPR violation, evidence of ownership, and your contact details. We will review and respond within 7 working days.
