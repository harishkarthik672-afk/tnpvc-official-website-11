Data Processing Principles

We process your data based on the following core values established by the council leadership:

  • Operational Integrity: Data is used purely for verifiable PVC project management.
  • Transparent Flow: Stakeholders are notified of any change in data handling.
  • Minimalist Collection: We never request sensitive financial data without legal escrow.
  • Encryption: Critical project blueprints and contract data are stored in encrypted silos.

Your Rights & Control

As a member of the TNPVC ecosystem, you retain ultimate ownership of your digitized identity. You can request a "Right to be Forgotten" or a data export at any point via our secure compliance channel.
