Customer Data Compliance

Our commitment to protecting your data and adhering to global and local standards.

Compliance Standards

TNPVC Council adheres to strict data compliance standards to ensure your personal and project information is handled with the utmost care and legality.

GDPR Ready

General Data Protection Regulation standards.

DPDP Act (India)

Compliant with Digital Personal Data Protection Act.

Data Processing Principles

We process your data based on the following core principles:

  • Lawfulness & Fairness: Data is collected only for specific, legitimate purposes.
  • Transparency: We are clear about what data we collect and why.
  • Data Minimization: We only collect the data we absolutely need.
  • Storage Limitation: We keep data only as long as necessary.

Your Rights & Control

You have full control over your data. You can request to view, edit, or delete your information at any time by contacting our compliance officer.
