Privacy Policy

How TNPVC Council collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last Updated: February 2026

Information We Collect

  • Name, contact number, and email address
  • Location data for service matching
  • Project details and communication history
  • Device and browsing information (via cookies)

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to match you with the right TNPVC service providers, to communicate about your projects, and to improve our platform experience.

We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

Data Sharing

Your information may be shared with verified TNPVC contractors or suppliers only as necessary to complete your project. All third parties are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Your Rights

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to opt out of marketing communications

Contact for Privacy

For any privacy-related queries or requests, please contact us through our Contact page. We respond to all privacy requests within 30 days.
