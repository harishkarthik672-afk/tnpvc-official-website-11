Building Digital Tamil Nadu

TNPVC connects Tamil Nadu's best contractors, workers, and fabricators with homeowners for quality uPVC and PVC work. Dexaz powers their digital future — building professional websites, apps, and digital tools so every tradesperson can grow their business beyond word-of-mouth.

The TNPVC Story

Srinivasan founded TNPVC Council to organise Tamil Nadu's uPVC and PVC industry — bringing verified contractors, skilled workers, and trusted suppliers together under one network. TNPVC ensures quality work, transparent pricing, and on-time delivery for every home project across all 38 districts.

The Dexaz Story

Siranthan Siran founded Dexaz as a technology company focused on building clean, powerful digital tools for growing businesses. When TNPVC's contractors and workers needed websites, apps, and an online presence, Dexaz stepped in — making digital growth simple, affordable, and effective for every tradesperson.
